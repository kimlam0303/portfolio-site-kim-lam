About Me

My Story

Hi I’m Kim. I am a UI designer currently based in Munich. I make clean, captivating, engaging and functional interfaces that help people’s lives. I started my creative endeavors playing various instruments such as classical piano, electric guitar (and the triangle), which is why I pursued music production and the musical art forms using Logic Pro X. I focussed on producing electronic music at first but then opened up to other styles as well such as band recordings or Hip Hop beats. Knowing that my passion lays in the realms of creativity I wanted to incorporate this drive into my professional life as well, which is why I found my way to UI design where my curiosity for giving form to the ideas of people can be perfectly applied. I spend a lot of time in front of the screen so therefore I am used to long and focused, creative working hours, figuring out the perfect solution to a problem.
