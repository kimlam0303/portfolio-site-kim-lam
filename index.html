Project 2 - KOOK.R

KOOK.R is a recipe app with social media features. Recipes are uploaded by the users on the internet, therefore offering a wide variety of international home cuisine. In this project I applied my UX skills to research competitor apps, interview users, create user personas and a user flow. For the UI part I made a moodboard, a style guide and finally the wireframes and mockups. The entire UI was then made responsive to fit the screen sizes of mobile, tablet, laptop and desktop devices.
